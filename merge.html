What is PDF Merge?

PDF Merge is a powerful tool that allows you to combine two or more PDF (Portable Document Format) files into a single cohesive document. Whether you are assembling reports, joining scanned pages, or organizing related documents, a PDF merger simplifies file management.

How to merge PDF files using ToolsPDF

Combining files is effortless with our tool. Just follow these steps:

  • Step 1: Click the "Select PDF Files" button or drag and drop your PDFs into the designated area.
  • Step 2: Once uploaded, you can drag the files to reorder them in your preferred sequence.
  • Step 3: Click the "Merge PDFs" button to combine the files instantly in your browser.
  • Step 4: Click the Download button to save the unified PDF file to your device.
