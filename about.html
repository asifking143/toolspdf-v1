About ToolsPDF

Empowering users with fast, secure, and private PDF tools directly in the browser.

Our Mission

At ToolsPDF, we believe that managing and manipulating your PDF documents shouldn't compromise your privacy or require expensive software subscriptions. Our mission is to provide a comprehensive suite of powerful, easy-to-use PDF tools that are accessible to everyone, everywhere, absolutely free of charge.

Why We Built This

The internet is filled with PDF utilities, but the vast majority of them require you to upload your sensitive, personal, or confidential documents to a remote cloud server. This poses significant privacy and security risks. We saw a better way. By leveraging modern web technologies (HTML5, JavaScript, and WebAssembly), we built a platform where your files are processed entirely on your own device.

100% Secure

Your files never leave your computer. Everything happens locally in your browser memory.

Lightning Fast

Because there are no files to upload or download from servers, processing is nearly instantaneous.

No Restrictions

No login walls, no premium tiers, no hidden fees. Just pure utility, right when you need it.
