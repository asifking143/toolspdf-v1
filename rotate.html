What is a PDF Rotator?

A PDF rotator allows you to fix upside down or sideways PDF pages. Whether you scanned a document in the wrong orientation or received a poorly formatted file, you can permanently rotate the pages to the correct viewing angle.

How to Rotate PDF Pages using ToolsPDF

  • Upload File: Select the PDF document you wish to correct.
  • Select Settings: Choose the rotation angle: 90 degrees clockwise, counter-clockwise, or 180 degrees.
  • Choose Pages: Apply the rotation to all pages simultaneously or select a custom page range for specific pages that need fixing.
  • Apply & Download: Click "Apply Rotation" to process the PDF directly on your device, then download the corrected file.

Benefits of ToolsPDF's Rotator

  • Completely Private: Processing happens entirely within your web browser. No files are uploaded to cloud servers.
  • Fast Fixing: Immediate turn-around time because data does not need to traverse the internet.
  • Precision: Rotate only the pages that require modification using custom ranges, leaving other pages untouched.
