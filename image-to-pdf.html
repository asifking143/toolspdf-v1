What is an Image to PDF Converter?

Image to PDF allows you to convert separate image files (such as photos, screenshots, or scanned documents) into a unified, multipage PDF document. This is particularly useful for sending a collection of pictures as a professional document attachment.

How to Convert Images to PDF using ToolsPDF

  • Step 1: Select your image files (JPG, PNG) by dragging and dropping them or clicking "Select Images".
  • Step 2: Drag the uploaded images to rearrange their sequence.
  • Step 3: Configure the page orientation (Auto, Portrait, or Landscape).
  • Step 4: Click "Create PDF". Your images will be joined instantly into a single file directly in your browser.
  • Step 5: Click "Download" to save the PDF.

Benefits of ToolsPDF's Converter

  • 100% Client-Side: Since your images are never sent over the internet to a server, this process is completely private and secure.
  • Fast & Free: No server queues mean your PDF generates instantly. Plus, there are no limitations or premium subscriptions.
  • Flexible Ordering: Easily drag and drop to set the exact order of pages in the output PDF.
