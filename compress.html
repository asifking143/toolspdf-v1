Why compress a PDF file?

PDF documents, especially those containing high-resolution images or scanned pages, can grow very large in file size. This makes them difficult to send via email, upload to submission portals, or share on messaging apps. A PDF compressor reduces the file size by optimizing the document's internal structure and removing unneeded metadata.

How to Compress PDF Size Online

  • Upload: Choose the PDF document you want to shrink from your computer or mobile device.
  • Process: Our client-side optimization engine will automatically streamline the document structure, dropping unused objects and metadata.
  • Download: Instantly download your new, lighter PDF, ready to be emailed or uploaded anywhere.

Secure and Private Compression

Unlike traditional PDF tools that make you wait while your sensitive documents are uploaded to a remote server, ToolsPDF performs the compression entirely within your web browser. This means your data never leaves your device, ensuring maximum privacy and blazing fast execution speeds.
