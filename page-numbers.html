Why Add Page Numbers to a PDF?

Adding page numbers to a lengthy PDF document improves readability and organization. It makes it easier for readers to navigate the content, reference specific sections, or organize printed handouts. Usually, PDF generators omit page numbers, but with our tool, you can easily insert them anywhere.

How to Insert Page Numbers Using ToolsPDF

  • Upload File: Select the PDF you want to modify.
  • Choose Position: Decide whether you want the numbers placed at the top or bottom, aligned left, right, or center.
  • Select Format: Pick a style such as "1", "Page 1", or "Page 1 of 5".
  • Configure: You can also set a custom starting number, or even skip numbering the cover page.
  • Apply & Download: Click "Add Page Numbers" to instantly apply the numbering locally directly in your web browser.
