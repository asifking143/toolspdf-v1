Why use our PDF Viewer?

While most modern browsers have built-in PDF viewers, you might be working on an older device or a restricted system. Our PDF viewer renders documents using Javascript natively on the canvas, providing a universally compatible way to read documents securely.

Completely Offline Reading Experience

When you choose a document to read, the file never actually leaves your computer. Our site instantly decodes and renders the pages straight into your screen using your device's memory. This is fundamentally safer compared to uploading sensitive files to cloud-based readers.
