What is PDF Split?

A PDF Splitter enables you to break apart a large PDF document into smaller, more manageable pieces. You can either extract a specific range of pages (like chapters of a book) or split every single page into an individual PDF file.

How to Split PDF files using ToolsPDF

  • Upload File: Drag and drop your PDF file or click to select from your device.
  • Select Mode: Choose "Extract Custom Range" to enter specific page numbers, or choose "Split All Pages" to convert each page to an independent file.
  • Split: Click the "Split PDF" button. Everything happens locally in your browser.
  • Download: Once complete, download your newly created PDF file or a ZIP archive containing all split files.

Benefits of using our Split tool

  • No Upload Required: Processing is done client-side, making it extremely secure for sensitive documents.
  • Flexible: Use comma-separated numbers and hyphen-separated ranges (e.g., 1, 3, 5-10) to extract exactly what you need.
